About me

Hi there — welcome to my business card.

My name is Norman T. Chella. I interview people, build communities, and create things. I'm the host of Podlovers Asia, RoamFM, AntiFool, Tempered Fables, and other shows.

I'm a big believer in podcasting, knowledge work, and polymathic thinking. I read far and wide.

Currently

Podcast Librarian at Podchaser — keeping up to date with credits and new podcasts, and building a relationship with every podcaster in the world. My role is to immortalise your work.

SuperLearner Certified Coach in memory and speed-reading techniques — helping high-performance autodidacts build Memory Palaces, train imagination, and remember everything.

Previously

Ex-Head of Marketing at a Japanese FinTech company · Chief of Business Development at Renegade Radio, Malaysia's premium podcasting network · Science Communications Director for the Planetary Health Lab, University of Edinburgh.
